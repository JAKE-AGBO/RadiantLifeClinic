Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Radiant Life Clinic offer?

Radiant Life Clinic offers a range of medical services, including primary care, specialty consultations, diagnostics, and preventive health programs.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our clinic, using our online booking system, or visiting our reception desk.

Do you accept insurance plans?

Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please check with our team or your insurance provider for specific coverage details.

What are the clinic's hours of operation?

We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

How do I prepare for a medical procedure?

Our medical team will provide specific instructions based on your procedure. Typically, fasting and specific medications may be involved.

What safety precautions are in place?

We prioritize your safety. Our clinic follows strict safety measures, including sanitization, mask requirements, and social distancing, to protect patients.

Can I request my medical records?

Yes, you can request your medical records through our patient portal or by contacting our medical records department.

Do you offer telehealth appointments?

Yes, we offer telehealth appointments for consultations, follow-ups, and non-emergency medical concerns.

What payment options are available?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and electronic funds transfers. Payment plans and financial assistance may be available for eligible patients.

Is Radiant Life Clinic accepting new patients?

Yes, we welcome new patients. Contact us to schedule your first appointment and join the Radiant Life Clinic family.
